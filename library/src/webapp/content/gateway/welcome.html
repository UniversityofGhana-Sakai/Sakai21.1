Welcome to University of Ghana Learning Management System

Sakai is a collaborative learning environment which is used at the University of Ghana to supplement traditional instruction, deliver online course materials, and facilitate collaboration between lecturers and students. Sakai is the primary Learning Management System (LMS) at University of Ghana for course management.

Researcher can collaborate on a project with colleagues on Sakai.

Lecturers may use Sakai to distribute class materials, communicate with their class, create assignments, and deliver online tests.

Students can track their courses, download course materials, submit their course work and communicate with their lecturers on Sakai .
